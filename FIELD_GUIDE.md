# PawPrint AI — Field Worker Guide

This guide explains how to use the PawPrint AI app for dog tracking in Nepal.

## 1. Install App on Android
1. Open Chrome on your phone.
2. Go to the app website URL.
3. Tap the **three dots** (top right) or the **Install App** popup.
4. Select **Add to Home Screen** or **Install**.
5. The app icon will now appear on your home screen.

## 2. Catch a Dog
1. Tap **Catch Dog** on the bottom navigation.
2. Tap the camera area to take a photo of the dog. Ensure the face and body are visible.
3. Select the dog's **Sex** (Male/Female/Unknown).
4. Select the **Age Group** (Puppy/Adult/Senior).
5. Select the **Condition** (Healthy/Injured/Critical).
6. Tag visual traits like **Ear Type** and **Coat Color**.
7. Tap **Save Catch Record**.

## 3. Identify a Dog
1. Tap **Identify Dog** on the bottom navigation.
2. Take a clear photo of the dog you just found.
3. Wait for the AI to find matches.
4. Compare the new photo with the "Original Catch" photo shown.
5. If it is the same dog, tap **Yes, this is the dog**.
6. If not, tap **Not a match** to see other results.

## 4. Offline Mode
* **No Internet?** No problem. You can still save catches.
* Catches will be stored on your phone automatically.
* When you get back to Wi-Fi or Data, the app will upload them in the background.
* Do not clear your browser cache while records are waiting to upload.
